# Holiday & Leave Management Feature

This specification details the **Holiday & Leave Management** feature, a cornerstone of the easyteams platform, designed to streamline time-off requests and approvals for small to medium-sized businesses. The feature aims to eliminate manual processes and fragmented tools, providing a unified and intuitive experience for both employees and managers.

## Purpose

The primary purpose is to offer a comprehensive system where employees can effortlessly manage their time off, and managers can efficiently oversee team availability and approve requests. This reduces administrative burden, improves transparency, and ensures compliance with company leave policies.

## Key Functionalities

1.  **Employee Leave Requests**: Employees can easily submit new leave requests by selecting dates, specifying the leave type (e.g., holiday, sick leave, paternity), and adding optional notes. The system will display their current leave balance to aid in planning.
2.  **Leave Balance Tracking**: Each employee will have a clear view of their remaining holiday allowance and other leave types, updated in real-time as requests are approved or denied.
3.  **Team Calendar View**: Managers and relevant team members can access a shared calendar displaying upcoming approved leave for their team, preventing staffing conflicts and ensuring adequate coverage.
4.  **Manager Approval Workflow**: Managers receive notifications for new leave requests. They can review all relevant details, including the employee's balance and team availability, before approving, denying, or requesting more information. Comments can be added for context.
5.  **Policy Configuration**: Admins can define company-wide holiday policies, including annual allowances, carry-over rules, and public holidays, ensuring consistency and adherence.

## User Stories Addressed

*   As an employee, I want to see my remaining holiday allowance so I can plan my vacations accurately.
*   As an employee, I want to request a holiday easily and see its approval status in real-time.
*   As a manager, I want to view my team's upcoming leave on a shared calendar to avoid staffing conflicts.
*   As a manager, I want to approve or deny leave requests quickly with all necessary context.

This feature directly addresses the problem of fragmented HR tools by consolidating leave management into a single, accessible platform, enhancing operational efficiency and employee experience.
