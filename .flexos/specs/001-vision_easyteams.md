# Project Vision: easyteams

## Overview
easyteams is designed to be the go-to platform for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) in France, aiming to simplify and optimize their daily team operations. The product focuses on streamlining internal processes, improving communication, and ensuring compliance with local regulations, particularly around employee leave.

## Target Users
-   **Small Business Owners/Managers in France:** Individuals responsible for overall team management, HR, and compliance. They need a tool to efficiently manage their workforce without getting bogged down by complex administrative tasks.
-   **Team Members/Employees:** Individuals who need to easily request time off, track their leave balances, and collaborate on projects.

## Problem Solved
Small businesses in France often struggle with the complexity of managing team operations, especially concerning employee holidays, which are governed by intricate and specific national and regional labor laws. This leads to administrative burden, potential non-compliance risks, and inefficiencies in team scheduling.

easyteams solves this by:
1.  **Simplifying Compliance:** Automating the application of French holiday regulations, reducing the risk of errors and legal issues.
2.  **Streamlining Operations:** Providing intuitive tools for task management, communication, and resource allocation.
3.  **Enhancing Transparency:** Offering clear overviews of team availability and project progress.

## Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
The core differentiator for easyteams is its sophisticated and compliant **Holiday Management System** tailored specifically for French labor laws. This includes:
-   Calculation of leave entitlements based on French legal standards.
-   Automated validation of holiday requests against company policies and legal limits.
-   Integration with public holidays and specific regional rules.
-   Clear reporting and audit trails for compliance.

## Core Features (Initial Scope)
1.  **Holiday Management:**
    *   Employee holiday requests and tracking.
    *   Manager approval workflows with compliance checks.
    *   Automated leave balance calculation.
    *   Integration with French public holidays and regional specifics.
2.  **Team Operations Dashboard:**
    *   Overview of team availability and pending holiday requests.
    *   Basic task visibility for managers.
3.  **Employee Directory:**
    *   Manage employee profiles and roles.

## Success Metrics
-   High adoption rate among French SMBs.
-   Reduction in administrative time spent on holiday management (e.g., 50% decrease).
-   Positive feedback on compliance and ease of use for holiday requests.
-   Increased efficiency in team scheduling and project delivery.
